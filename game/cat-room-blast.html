Cat Room Blast

Fullscreen

Cat Room Blast is a delightful match-3 puzzle game where you help a group of adorable cats renovate and decorate their dream home. Combine colorful tiles to complete levels, earn rewards, and unlock new rooms to customize with charming furniture and decorations. As you progress, you’ll uncover the unique personalities of each cat and their stories, making the game as heartwarming as it is fun.

How to Play

  1. Match Tiles: Swap adjacent tiles to create a match of three or more of the same type. Matched tiles will disappear, allowing new tiles to fall into place.

  2. Complete Objectives: Each level has specific objectives, such as clearing a certain number of tiles, collecting items, or breaking obstacles. Achieve these goals within a limited number of moves to progress.

  3. Earn Stars: Completing levels rewards you with stars, which are used to unlock and customize new rooms in the cats’ house.

  4. Decorate Rooms: Use the stars you've earned to choose from various decorations and furniture to design the rooms according to your style.

  5. Power-Ups: Match more than three tiles to create special power-ups, like bombs or rockets, which can clear larger areas of the board.

  6. Story Progression: As you advance, you'll unlock new chapters in the cats’ story, revealing more about their backgrounds and relationships.

Controls

  • Mouse/Touch Controls: Use your mouse or tap on the screen to select and swap tiles.
  • Drag and Drop: Click and hold on a tile, then drag it to swap with an adjacent tile.
  • Power-Ups: Activate power-ups by clicking or tapping on them once they appear on the board.

FAQs

1. How do I get more stars?

  • Earn stars by completing levels. The number of stars awarded depends on the level's difficulty and your performance.

2. Can I change the room decorations after selecting them?

  • Yes, you can always go back and change the decorations in any room by using the stars you’ve earned.

3. What happens if I run out of moves?

  • If you run out of moves before completing the level objectives, you can use in-game currency to purchase additional moves or replay the level.

4. Are there daily challenges or events?

  • Yes, the game features daily challenges and special events that offer unique rewards and extra stars.

5. How do I unlock new rooms?

  • New rooms are unlocked by completing a certain number of levels or by earning enough stars. Each room comes with its own set of customization options.

6. Can I play offline?

  • Yes, Cat Room Blast can be played offline, but some features like daily challenges and events require an internet connection.

7. How do I use power-ups?

  • Power-ups are automatically created when you match more than three tiles. To use them, simply click or tap on the power-up, and it will activate.

8. Is there a way to sync progress across devices?

  • Yes, by connecting the game to your social media or email account, you can sync your progress across multiple devices.

9. What should I do if the game crashes or freezes?

  • Try restarting the game or your device. If the issue persists, check for updates or contact customer support through the game’s help section.

10. Are there in-app purchases?

  • Yes, Cat Room Blast offers in-app purchases for additional lives, power-ups, and other in-game items. However, these are optional and not necessary to progress in the game.
